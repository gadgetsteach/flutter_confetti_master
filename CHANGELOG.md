# Changelog

## 1.0.0

* Initial release of `flutter_confetti_master`.
* Premium 60 FPS Canvas celebration and particle physics engine.
* Support for 15 distinct confetti and celebration animation effects:
  1. Explosion Burst
  2. Cannon Shot
  3. Rain Confetti
  4. Fireworks
  5. Star Confetti
  6. Heart Confetti
  7. Emoji Confetti
  8. Spiral Motion
  9. Wave Motion
  10. Advanced Particle Engine
  11. Money Rain
  12. Balloon Burst (Interactive Pop)
  13. Sparkle Trail
  14. Trophy Celebration
  15. Rocket Launch Effect
* Custom color configurations, particle count, gravity, speed range, and emission duration.
* Auto-play, loop, pause, resume, stop, and manual blast trigger controls.
* Complete interactive Example Showcase application with parameter tuning.
* Extensive unit tests, widget tests, and code coverage.
